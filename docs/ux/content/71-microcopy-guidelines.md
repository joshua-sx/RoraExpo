# 71 — Microcopy Guidelines
## Tone
- Calm.
- Direct.
- No urgency unless real.
## Rules
- Avoid blame language.
- Avoid guilt copy.
- Keep confirmations explicit.
## Examples
- Good: “Looking for drivers…”
- Good: “Confirm driver”
- Bad: “Hurry! Only 10 seconds left!”
